# Gambit Go-To-Market

Stage: live app, ~35 signups, 11 real stakers. This is a "first 1,000 true
players" plan, not paid acquisition. Distribution through channels we do not pay
for. Paid ads now would burn cash on people who bounce.

## The wedge
Lead marketing with **Naija Whot, in Nigeria, for small real money**. Whot needs
zero rules education and it is culturally ours. One sentence a trader gets: play
Whot, win real money, cash out to your phone. Other games stay in the app; the
*marketing* leads with Whot. Win the room we can win.

## ICP
18-30, Nigeria first (then Kenya, Ghana). Already has MiniPay / Opera Mini.
Plays mobile games, sends small money, on X + TikTok + WhatsApp. Does NOT own
crypto and does not want to. Privy embedded wallet is why they can play. Lead
with "no crypto needed", never "on Celo".

## The growth loop (wire every feature into this)
1. Join the FREE Weekly Cup (no money, humans only, deadline). Top of funnel.
2. Podium makes winners post on X / WhatsApp for a bonus.
3. That post carries their referral link. A friend taps it.
4. Friend plays free, gets daily G$, builds a streak.
5. Friend stakes 0.10 for a real match. Now in the money loop.
6. Back to 1.
Free + leaderboard + deadline is the most shareable structure in gaming.

## Channels, ranked
1. **MiniPay directory** — tens of millions of users in our exact market, the
   wallet already installed. The submission matters more than any ad. Everything
   else bridges until this lands.
2. **WhatsApp groups** — where Nigeria lives. Universal share drops a link into
   football/campus/betting groups. One engaged person in ten groups > a billboard.
3. **X as a daily engine** — weekly "score to beat, N days left" with the live
   leaderboard; every settle post winners + payout tx; reply to every bragged
   score; quote-tweet Celo/MiniPay/GoodDollar so their teams amplify.
4. **Micro-creators (5k-30k)** — 10 Nigerian gaming/Whot/make-money creators do
   "I won X playing Whot" for a small cut or a sponsored cup. Beats one expensive post.
5. **Celo ecosystem** — keep submitting to Proof of Ship (placed 7/50 in June);
   ask GoodDollar to feature us for using G$ + GoodID.

## 30-day plan
- Wk1: ship podium share loop + leaderboard names; submit MiniPay; set X cadence.
- Wk2: run one funded free cup (~25 USDm), push in 20 WhatsApp groups + X. Spike to screenshot.
- Wk3: 5 micro-creators, a sponsored cup each; make referral leaderboard public.
- Wk4: double the channel that moved; kill the rest.

## Metrics that matter
Week-2 return rate; free-to-staked conversion; referral k-factor (>0.5 compounds);
cup participation week over week. Ignore raw signups.

## Do NOT
Call it gambling/betting (Celo avoid-lists it). Run paid ads yet. Chase 5
countries. Lead with crypto/Celo/web3. Lead with: play Whot, win money, cash out
to your phone.

One line: get listed in MiniPay, make the free Weekly Cup the weekly thing people
share into WhatsApp and X, let referrals turn each winner into three new players,
all aimed at Nigerians who already know Whot.
